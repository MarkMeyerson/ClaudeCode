## Phase 1: ASSESS - Understanding Your AI Readiness

### What is the Assessment?

The AI Readiness Assessment evaluates your organization across **12 critical dimensions**:

1. **Digital Maturity** - Your current digital capabilities
2. **AI Readiness** - Preparedness for AI adoption
3. **Data Capabilities** - Data quality, accessibility, governance
4. **Organizational Culture** - Innovation mindset and leadership
5. **Technical Infrastructure** - IT systems and architecture
6. **Process Automation** - Current automation maturity
7. **Skills & Talent** - AI expertise and capabilities
8. **Competitive Landscape** - Market position and pressure
9. **Regulatory Compliance** - Legal and compliance readiness
10. **Financial Readiness** - Budget and investment capacity
11. **Change Management** - Ability to manage transformation
12. **Vendor Ecosystem** - Partner and vendor relationships

### Starting Your Assessment

**Step 1: Access the Assessment**

- Click "Assessments" in the left navigation
- Click "Start New Assessment"
- Select assessment type:
  - **Comprehensive (Recommended):** Full 250-question assessment (2-3 hours)
  - **Quick:** Abbreviated 50-question assessment (30 minutes)
  - **Focused:** Target specific dimensions (45 minutes)

**Step 2: Answer Questions Honestly**

- There are no "wrong" answers
- Honesty ensures accurate recommendations
- You can save progress and continue later
- Add evidence and documentation when available
- Use the confidence slider to indicate certainty

**Step 3: Collaborate with Your Team**

The assessment works best as a collaborative effort:

```
Recommended Participants:
- Executive Sponsor (CEO, COO)
- IT/Technology Leader (CTO, CIO)
- Data/Analytics Leader
- Operations Leader
- Finance Leader
- HR/People Leader
```

**Tip:** Invite team members using the "Collaborate" button!

### Understanding Your Results

Once submitted, you'll receive:

#### 1. Overall Readiness Score (0-100)

- **85-100:** Advanced - Excellent AI readiness
- **70-84:** Developing - Good foundation, some gaps
- **50-69:** Emerging - Basic capabilities, significant work needed
- **30-49:** Beginning - Limited capabilities, major investment required
- **0-29:** Initial - Minimal AI readiness

#### 2. Dimension Scores

Detailed breakdown across all 12 dimensions with:
- Individual scores
- Maturity level
- Strengths and weaknesses
- Specific recommendations

#### 3. SWOT Analysis

**Strengths:** What you're doing well
**Weaknesses:** Areas needing improvement
**Opportunities:** AI use cases aligned with goals
**Threats:** Risks and challenges to address

#### 4. Quick Wins

3-5 high-impact, low-effort initiatives you can start immediately:
- **Example:** Deploy AI chatbot for customer support
- **Example:** Automate email classification
- **Example:** Implement document processing automation

#### 5. Strategic Recommendations

10+ prioritized recommendations with:
- Implementation timeline
- Resource requirements
- Expected ROI

#### 6. Benchmark Comparison

See how you compare to:
- Industry peers

## Common Questions (FAQ)

### Getting Started

**Q: How long does the assessment take?**
A: The comprehensive assessment takes 2-3 hours. You can save progress and complete it over multiple sessions.

**Q: Can multiple people contribute to one assessment?**
A: Yes! We recommend collaborative assessments. You can invite team members to contribute their perspectives.

**Q: How often should we do assessments?**
A: We recommend re-assessing every 6-12 months to track progress.

### Projects and Implementation

**Q: How many pilot projects should we start with?**
A: We recommend 2-3 pilot projects to start. This allows you to learn and build momentum without overwhelming your team.

**Q: What if a pilot project fails?**
A: Pilot projects are learning opportunities. Document lessons learned and apply them to future initiatives. Not every experiment succeeds.

**Q: How long should a pilot project last?**
A: Most successful pilots run 8-12 weeks. Long enough to prove value, short enough to maintain momentum.

### Measuring Success

**Q: How quickly should we expect to see ROI?**
A: Quick wins can show ROI in 1-3 months. More complex initiatives may take 6-12 months. The platform helps you track and forecast ROI.

**Q: What's a good ROI target for AI projects?**
A: Most successful AI projects deliver 200-500% ROI within the first year. Start with realistic targets and exceed them.

**Q: How do we measure "soft" benefits like employee satisfaction?**
A: The platform includes user satisfaction surveys, NPS tracking, and qualitative feedback collection.

### Support and Training

**Q: What if I need help using the platform?**
A: Multiple support channels:
- In-app help center
- Video tutorials
- Email support (support@sherpatech.ai)
- Live chat (Business hours)
- Weekly office hours with consultants

**Q: Are training sessions recorded?**
A: Yes! All training sessions are recorded and available in the Training Center.

**Q: Can we request custom training?**
A: Absolutely! Contact your consultant to arrange custom training sessions.
